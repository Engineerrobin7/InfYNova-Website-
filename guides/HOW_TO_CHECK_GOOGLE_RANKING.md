1. Go to: https://search.google.com/search-console

3. Enter your domain (e.g., infynova.in)
4. Verify ownership (add verification code to your site)
5. Submit sitemap: https://yourdomain.com/sitemap.xml

**What You'll See:**

- Total clicks
- Total impressions
- Click-through rate (CTR)
- Which pages rank best

**Timeline:**
- Setup: 5 minutes

- Full data: 7 days

**This is the OFFICIAL Google tool. Use this!**
